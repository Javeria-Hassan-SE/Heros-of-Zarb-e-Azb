First Press Release
About Launching of
Operation Zarb-E-Azb

Rawalpindi




Pakistan Army has launched a highly needed operation in North Waziristan , according to an ISPR Press Release.

DG ISPR has said that on the directions of the government, Armed Forces Of Pakistan have launched a comprehensive operation against foreign and local terrorist who are hiding in sanctuarities in North Waziristan Agency.The Operation has named as operation "Zarb-E-Azb"said the Press Release.

 Defence Minister

Khwja Asif

said the government went through with peace talks, but once they failed the government decided to launch military action.he went to an add that civilian residings in that area will face difficulties due to operation, but the government is prepared to assist them.

"The Army is ready and citizens should remain calm army is prepared to deal with difficult situations and will help citizens move",

he said, adding that air and ground forces are fully ready to act and will bring complete peace to the area.

"We Will Complete the operation as soon as possible,"

he added. The decision to launch an operation comes exactly a week after the all-night siege of karachi Airport that killed 38 people,including 10 attackers,and all but destroyed a tentative peace process between the TTP and te government .The Announcements also comes after jets bombed militants hideouts in the region killing 80 militants, according to ISPR. The Operation was named as Zarb-E-Azb which means

"Sharp & Cutting"

.It's reportedly the sword used by Prophet Muhammad (صَلَّى اللّٰهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّمَ) in the battle of Badar.
